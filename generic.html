A bit about Myself

Hey, I'm Conor Warrilow.

At the end of 2023 I graduated from the University of Western Australia with a Bachelors in Mathematics and statistics specializing in statistics and probability. Over the last year and a half I've developed an extensive skill set outside of university to compliment my studies and prepare for a career in data. Combined with my strong background in statistical theory, these skills provide me with the ability to translate complex questions into simple insights for a diverse range of industries.

Talking about myself some more

1. I'm passionate about AI technology and its current and future impacts.
2. I'm a curious person who enjoys learning from other people with different and unique life experiences.
3. When I'm not working with data, I love to do photography, play the drums, and discover new foods.
